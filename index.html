Joanne, Elise, and Meredith thank you for your purchase of The Dream Blanket! Each book helps toward purchasing another book as well as fabric to make blankets to donate to other children! Create Magic In Your Community

We are just beginning our journey to donate books and blankets. Our first donations have gone to shelters in Hyannis, Massachusetts, Portland, Maine and Bluffton, South Carolina. We would love to see our Dream Blanket Project go into many communities. If you are interested in helping by making blankets and/or buying books, please email us for information.
